We've been hard at work releasing new features and enhancing Faved, and I'm excited to share all the recent updates with you. These changes were designed to make accessing and navigating your web links faster and smoother.

## New table layout

![Table layout screenshot](/static/images/posts/new-table-layout-and-streamlined-toolbar/GyfczVaxo1ZqU6a.png)

Our new table layout is created to speed up your workflow like never before. With customizable columns, it helps reduce clutter and boost productivity.

## Improved list view
![List layout screenshot](/static/images/posts/new-table-layout-and-streamlined-toolbar/xchiNq1niYfc9Yh.png)

Experience a completely redesigned list interface with enhanced readability and visual hierarchy.

## Sticky, Streamlined Toolbar
Our redesigned toolbar stays visible as you scroll, featuring a cleaner layout with faster access to your most-used functions.

## Customizable Field Visibility
You can now show or hide specific fields, creating the perfect custom view for your workflow. Focus on what matters most to you.

## Major Frontend Refactors Under the Hood
While you won't see these changes directly, you'll definitely feel them! Improved performance, faster loading times, and a more responsive interface thanks to our comprehensive frontend optimization.
