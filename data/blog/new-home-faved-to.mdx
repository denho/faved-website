We've brought Faved under one roof. From today, everything lives on a single domain:

* 🌐 The website you're reading is now at **faved.to** (previously faved.dev)
* ☁️ Faved Cloud has moved to **app.faved.to** (previously faved.cloud)

## Why the change?

As Faved has grown, having the project spread across separate `.dev` and `.cloud` addresses started to feel disjointed. One brand, two unrelated domains — it made the project harder to remember and harder to talk about.

Moving everything under **faved.to** fixes that. The marketing site, the docs, and the cloud app now share a single, recognizable home, with the app sitting neatly at `app.faved.to`. It's a small change on the surface, but it makes Faved feel like the one cohesive product it actually is.

## Do I need to do anything?

**Mostly nothing.** Old domains redirect automatically, so every existing link keeps working — you'll just land on the new address.

Faved Cloud users may be prompted to sign in again on their first visit to the new app address (<a href={siteMetadata.appUrl}>app.faved.to</a>). If you use the PWA, the Apple Shortcut, or the bookmarklet, it's worth reinstalling them to point to the new domain so they work faster without relying on the redirect.

And if you self-host, nothing changes for you at all. Your instance runs on your own domain and isn't affected by this move.
