Faved is built to stay fast even with large collections, with several ways to find and view your saved
links.

## Instant search

Start typing and results appear instantly. Search matches a bookmark's **url, title, description, or
notes**, so you can locate what you need right away even in big collections.

## Filtering by tags

Select one or more tags from the sidebar to narrow the list to just those bookmarks. With
[rollups](/docs/guides/organizing-with-tags#rollups), choosing a parent tag also includes everything
filed under its child tags. See [Organizing with tags](/docs/guides/organizing-with-tags) for how to
build and manage your tag hierarchy.

## Sorting

Sort your bookmarks by **creation date, last updated date, title, description, URL, or notes** to
surface the most relevant or most recent links at the top. Click the same field again to flip the
direction between ascending and descending.

## Pagination

Move through large collections with simple pagination controls that show where you are and let you jump
straight to the page you want.

## Layouts

Switch between layouts to suit the task at hand:

- **List view** — a clean, readable list with clear visual hierarchy.
- **Card view** — a visual, image-forward grid that automatically adapts to your screen size, fitting as
  many items as possible on any device.
- **Table view** — a dense, productivity-focused layout with **customizable columns**.

You can **show or hide specific fields** to build the exact view that fits your workflow, and the
**sticky toolbar** stays visible as you scroll for fast access to your most-used controls.

## Stateful URLs

Your search query, selected tags, sorting, and pagination are all captured in the URL, so your view is
fully preserved. Bookmark to reopen the exact same view later — and because every
change updates the URL, your browser's **back** and **forward** buttons step through the views you've
visited.
