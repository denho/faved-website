Instead of managing your library one bookmark at a time, you can select as many bookmarks as you like
and act on them in a single operation.

## Available bulk actions

- **Delete** all the selected bookmarks at once.
- **Add, update or remove tags** across every selected bookmark.
- **Refetch metadata** for every selected item, refreshing titles, descriptions, and preview images in
  one go.

## When bulk actions help most

Bulk actions are perfect for cleaning up after a large import, restructuring tags across multiple links, or
removing bookmarks you no longer need — all without having to perform the same action over and over again.
