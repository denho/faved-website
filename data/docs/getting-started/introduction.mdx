## What is Faved?

Faved is a powerful, free, and open-source bookmark manager that you host yourself. It's designed to help you organize, categorize, and access your saved web content from anywhere, on any device.

What sets Faved apart from traditional cloud-based bookmark services is that your data remains entirely under your control. There's no third-party service collecting your browsing habits, no risk of your bookmarks disappearing if a company shuts down, and no subscription fees. Your bookmarks, your rules.

## Key Features

- **Self-Hosted & Private**: Deploy Faved on your own computer, server, or cloud infrastructure. Your bookmarks never leave your control, ensuring complete privacy and data ownership.

- **Open Source**: Fully transparent codebase that you can inspect, customize, and extend. Join a community of contributors making Faved better every day.

- **Advanced Tagging System**: Go beyond simple folders with a powerful nested tagging system. Apply custom styling to tags, pin frequently used tags to the top, and create a personalized organizational structure that works for you.

- **Responsive Design**: Access your bookmarks seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices with a unified, responsive interface—no separate mobile apps required.

- **Lightning Fast Performance**: Built for speed with optimized database queries and efficient caching. Enjoy instant search and navigation even with thousands of bookmarks.

## Who is Faved For?

Faved is the perfect solution for:

- **Privacy Advocates**: Keep your browsing history and bookmark collection completely private without relying on third-party cloud services.

- **Researchers & Academics**: Manage extensive reference libraries with advanced tagging and categorization that scales to thousands of sources.

- **Developers & Technical Professionals**: Organize documentation, tutorials, code snippets, and technical resources with a system built by developers, for developers.

- **Power Users**: If you've ever lost bookmarks to a service shutdown, migration nightmare, or sudden price increase, Faved gives you permanent ownership and control.

- **Teams & Organizations**: Deploy a shared bookmark repository for your team while maintaining full control over access and data security.
