## What is Faved?

Faved is a powerful bookmark manager that helps you organize, categorize, and access your saved web content from anywhere, on any device.

There are two ways to use it:

- **Self-host it for free.** Faved is open-source and runs on your own computer, server, or cloud infrastructure, keeping your data entirely under your control — no third party collecting your habits, no risk of your bookmarks vanishing if a company shuts down.
- **Or let us host it for you.** [Faved Cloud](https://faved.cloud/signup) is a fully managed version that's ready in under a minute, with automatic backups, updates, and dedicated support. Start with a 14-day free trial — no setup or maintenance required.

Choose the privacy and ownership of self-hosting, or the convenience of the cloud. Under the hood, both run the same Faved, allowing seamless migration between Cloud and self-hosted versions whenever you wish — your bookmarks, tags, and workflow transfer completely.

## Key Features

- **Advanced Tagging System**: Go beyond simple folders with a powerful nested tagging system. Customize tags with colors, pin frequently used tags to the top, and create a personalized organizational structure that works for you.

- **Smart Bookmark Management**: Automatic fetching of titles, descriptions, and preview images, plus built-in duplicate detection to keep your collection clean.

- **Instant Search & Sorting**: Find bookmarks as you type, with flexible sorting across collections of any size.

- **Bulk Actions**: Delete, retag, and refetch metadata across many bookmarks at once.

- **Works on Any Device**: Fully responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile, and installable as a PWA for a near-native mobile experience—no separate apps required.

- **Light & Dark Mode**: Automatically syncs with your system theme for comfortable viewing in any lighting condition.

- **Integrations**: Save links from any browser with a lightweight bookmarklet, or send them via the native Share menu on iPhone, Mac, and iPad.

- **Import & Migration**: Import from Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge with folder structure preserved, and migrate from Raindrop.io, Pocket, and other tools retaining collections and tags.

- **Open Source**: Fully transparent codebase that you can inspect, customize, and extend.
