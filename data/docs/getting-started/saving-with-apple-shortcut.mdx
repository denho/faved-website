The Apple Shortcuts integration lets you send links straight to Faved without leaving the app you're
in. Tap **Share** in Safari, your RSS reader, or anywhere else on your Apple device, pick **Add to Faved**, and the page
lands in your library. On Mac, the same shortcut is also a click
away in the **Services menu**.

## Setting up the shortcut


> [!TIP]
> Ensure iCloud sync is enabled for the Shortcuts app to have it available on all your Apple devices.

        Visit <a href={siteMetadata.appleShortcutUrl} target="_blank" className="break-all">{siteMetadata.appleShortcutUrl}</a> and click **Get Shortcut** on the
        opened page. You'll be redirected to the Apple Shortcuts app to proceed with the setup.

    </Step>

    <Step title="Begin setup in the Shortcuts app">

        In the Apple Shortcuts app, click **Add Shortcut...** on Mac, or **Set Up Shortcut** on
        iPhone/iPad.

        [//]: # (![Add Shortcut screen in the Apple Shortcuts app]&#40;/static/images/docs/apple-shortcuts/add-shortcut.png&#41;)

    </Step>

    <Step title="Enter your Faved URL">

        When prompted, enter your Faved instance URL. If you're using the cloud version, keep the default, `https://faved.cloud`.

    </Step>

    <Step title="Confirm to finish">

        Click **Add Shortcut** to confirm. The **Add to Faved** shortcut is added to your Shortcuts library, and the **Add to Faved** action now appears in your Share Sheet — and in the Services menu on Mac.

    </Step>

</Steps>


## Saving a link

### Using the Share Sheet
    <video
        controls={false}
        autoPlay
        loop
        muted
        playsInline
        className="mx-auto w-full max-w-xs rounded-lg"
    >
        <source src="/static/videos/posts/saving-with-apple-shortcut/using-shortcut-ios.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
    </video>
<Steps>

    <Step title="Open the webpage">

        Open the webpage you want to save in Safari or another browser on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

    </Step>

    <Step title="Open the Share Sheet">

        Tap or click the browser's **Share** button.

        On Mac, you can also use the app's
        **File → Share** menu.

        <img
            src="/static/images/posts/saving-with-apple-shortcut/file-share-menu-macos.png"
            alt="File → Share menu on macOS"
            className="w-full rounded-lg"
            style={{ maxWidth: '400px' }}
        />
    </Step>

    <Step title="Choose Add to Faved">

        Select **Add to Faved** to send the current page or link to Faved.

        On Mac's Share menu,
        choose **Shortcuts** first, then **Add to Faved**.

        [//]: # (![Add to Faved shortcut highlighted in the iOS share sheet]&#40;/static/images/docs/apple-shortcuts/share-sheet.png&#41;)

        > [!TIP]
        > On Mac, if the shortcut doesn't appear in the Share menu, check **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Extensions → Sharing** and ensure **Shortcuts** is enabled.


    </Step>

    <Step title="Add details and save">

    Optionally, add notes and tags, then click **Save**. The webpage will be stored and available in Faved.

    </Step>

</Steps>

### Using the Services menu (Mac)

<Steps>

    <Step title="Select a link">

        Highlight any visible URL in any app, or right-click a selected link in your browser.

    </Step>

    <Step title="Open the Services menu">

        Open the **Services** menu and select **Add to Faved** to send the selected link to Faved.

    </Step>

    <Step title="Add details and save">

        Optionally, add notes and tags, then click **Save**.  The selected link will be stored and available in Faved.

    </Step>

</Steps>
